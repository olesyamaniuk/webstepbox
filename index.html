Extended FAQ

  • How are workshops different from normal courses?

    WSX box uses gamification, live challenges, and group activities instead of lectures.

  • Do I need special software?

    No, everything runs via browser or mobile app.

  • Can I join as an individual?

    Yes! You can join solo and get matched into teams, or sign up as a company group.

  • What makes WSX box better than classic e-learning?

    It’s interactive, social, and mission-driven — you learn by doing.

  • Can companies book private sessions?

    Yes, corporate packages with tailored workshops are available.

  • How much time per week is needed?

    Average 2–4 hours for individuals, 5–6 hours for Premium teams.
